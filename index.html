✓ Our Solution

Colors become patterns.

Based on Dual Coding Theory, we add a unique visual pattern to every color so content is readable by shape alone — not just hue. This improves comprehension by 20% for everyone, not just people with CVD.

🔴 Red

Diagonal stripes — signaling intensity

🟢 Green

Dot grid — signaling stability

🔵 Blue

Wave pattern — signaling flow

🟡 Yellow

Checkerboard — signaling caution

Grounded in UC Berkeley Digital Accessibility Program guidelines · WCAG 2.1 compliant · 20+ patterns in the free library

⚙ How It Works

AI in 3 Steps

Unlike consumer apps that apply filters over the screen, BeyondColor uses a multi-stage computer vision pipeline to edit specific visual elements inside your files.

01
🔍

Layout Analysis

PubLayNet model distinguishes text paragraphs — which don't need changing — from charts and diagrams, which do.

PubLayNet
02
✂️

Precise Segmentation

Segment Anything Model (SAM) isolates specific shapes — individual bars of a graph, zones on a map — with pixel-perfect accuracy.

SAM
03
🎨

Pattern Injection

Colors are analyzed in LMS color space. When conflicts are found, standardized geometric patterns are overlaid. A red wire becomes the "striped wire" — identifiable by everyone.

OpenCV
⚡ Average processing time: under 3 seconds per document

🚀 Expansion Roadmap

From Almaty to the world

Rapid validation, infrastructure integration, then global reach to meet rising accessibility mandates.

◎
Phase 1 · 2025–2026

Validation

5 schools in Almaty by September 2026. Closed alpha with 20 STEM teachers. Target: under 3s processing, 95%+ accuracy.

🚀
Phase 2 · 2026–2027

Integration

API integration with Kundelik, reaching 3.8M students across Kazakhstan. Expand to Dnevnik.ru and the CIS region.

🌐
Phase 3 · 2027+

Global

US & Europe by 2027, meeting European Accessibility Act mandates and US DOJ ADA Title II requirements for WCAG 2.1.

Months 1–3 · Optimization & Alpha

Build & Test

Refine PubLayNet + SAM pipeline. Closed alpha with 20 STEM teachers. Track: <3s processing, 95% accuracy targets.

Months 4–6 · The Pilot

Prove It Works

Partner with 3 high schools in Biology, Chemistry, and Geography. Measure exam error rate reduction for CVD students.

Months 7–12 · B2B Launch

Go to Market

Armed with pilot data, secure first API licensing deal with Kundelik for the upcoming academic year.
